## 🎯 Logo Guidelines

### Size Recommendations
- **Small (10-15%)**: Subtle branding
- **Medium (15-20%)**: Balanced (recommended)
- **Large (20-30%)**: Bold branding

### File Types
- ✅ PNG with transparency
- ✅ SVG for scalability
- ✅ JPG with white background
- ❌ Avoid complex logos

### Best Practices
1. Use square or circular logos
2. Ensure logo has good contrast
3. Test scanning with logo
4. Keep logo simple
5. Use transparent backgrounds

## 🌈 Color Contrast Guide

### High Contrast (Best)
- Dark primary + Light background
- Light primary + Dark background
- Contrast ratio > 4.5:1

### Medium Contrast (Good)
- Colored primary + Light background
- Contrast ratio 3:1 - 4.5:1

### Low Contrast (Avoid)
- Similar colors
- Contrast ratio < 3:1
- May not scan reliably

## 📱 Testing Checklist

Before finalizing your QR code:

- [ ] Scans on iPhone
- [ ] Scans on Android
- [ ] Scans in bright light
- [ ] Scans in dim light
- [ ] Scans at arm's length
- [ ] Logo doesn't block scanning
- [ ] Colors have good contrast
- [ ] Frame text is readable
- [ ] Correct destination URL
- [ ] Downloaded in correct format

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### QR Code Won't Scan
- ✅ Increase contrast
- ✅ Reduce logo size
- ✅ Use simpler pattern
- ✅ Test with different apps

### Logo Looks Bad
- ✅ Use transparent background
- ✅ Reduce size
- ✅ Use simpler logo
- ✅ Ensure square aspect ratio

### Colors Look Wrong
- ✅ Check color contrast
- ✅ Test on different screens
- ✅ Use preset colors
- ✅ Avoid very light colors

### Frame Text Cut Off
- ✅ Keep text under 30 characters
- ✅ Use shorter words
- ✅ Try different frame style

## 📊 Performance Tips

- Use templates for fastest results
- Upload logos < 1MB
- Test before mass printing
- Save customization settings
- Use high error correction
